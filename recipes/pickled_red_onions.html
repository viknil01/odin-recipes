Pickled Red Onions

Description

Pickled red onions are a food item consisting of red onions pickled in a solution of vinegar and salt, often with other preservatives and flavourings.

  • 1,5 dl water
  • 0,75 dl sugar
  • 0,5 dl vinegar
  • 2 red onions

Steps

  1. Heat up the water, sugar and vinegar until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Peel and slice the red onions into thin circles.
  3. Add the onions to the vinegar solution and let rest for at least 20 min before serving.
